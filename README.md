## 🏆 Victorys Game Jam

**HEARTBOUND** was developed during the **Victorys Game Jam**, built around the jam theme:

> **Endless Origins**

The project was designed and developed within the game jam time limit, focusing on creating a replayable roguelite experience inspired by Egyptian mythology.

---

# 📖 Overview

**HEARTBOUND** is a 2D Roguelite where every playthrough is unique.

Take control of **Anubis**, guardian of the dead, as he journeys through the ever-changing **Duat** to uncover the truth behind the forgotten souls and confront **Set**, who seeks to reshape the balance of the afterlife.

Each run features:

- Random starting weapons
- Procedurally generated rooms
- Divine blessings from Egyptian gods
- Puzzle encounters
- Fast-paced combat
- Meta progression

Every death sends the player back to the beginning, but every run reveals new strategies and new stories.

# 🎮 Gameplay

### Core Loop

```text
Home Base
     │
     ▼
Receive Random Weapon
     │
     ▼
Explore Random Rooms
     │
     ├── Combat
     ├── Puzzle
     ├── God Room
     ├── Story Event
     └── Shop
     │
     ▼
Boss Battle
     │
     ▼
Next World
     │
     ▼
Return Home
Upgrade
Repeat
```

# ⚔️ Combat

The player combines:

- Light Attack
- Heavy Attack
- Dash
- Divine Boons
- Weapon Abilities

Every weapon offers a completely different playstyle, encouraging experimentation across multiple runs.

---

# 👑 Divine Boons

Throughout the journey, Egyptian gods grant powerful blessings.

Boons can:

- Increase damage
- Improve movement
- Enhance survivability
- Unlock special combat effects
- Modify abilities

Because the available blessings change every run, players are encouraged to create different builds.

---

# 🏺 Weapons

Every run begins with a randomly selected weapon.

Current weapon types include:

- Sword
- Spear
- Axe
- Twin Daggers
- Magic Staff

Each weapon has its own strengths and combat style.

---

# 🧩 Room Types

The Duat is divided into multiple room types.

### ⚔️ Combat Room

Fight enemies and earn rewards.

### 👑 God Room

Receive a blessing from an Egyptian god.

### 🧩 Puzzle Room

Solve environmental puzzles to obtain valuable rewards.

### 🛒 Shop

Spend collected coins on upgrades and healing.

### 📖 Story Room

Discover lore about the Duat and forgotten souls.

### 💀 Boss Room

Face powerful enemies guarding the next world.

---

# 🌍 World Progression

The journey consists of:

- 3 Worlds
- 15 Total Rooms
- Randomized Encounters
- Different Enemy Types
- Boss Battle at the end of every world

---

# 📖 Story

Something has gone terribly wrong in the Duat.

Souls without tombs, wealth, or proper burial rites wander endlessly through the afterlife.

Set believes the gods have abandoned them.

As **Anubis**, you must descend into the unstable Duat, uncover forgotten truths, and decide whether justice should preserve the ancient order—or change forever.
